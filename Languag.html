Culture & heritage

Language lives in culture

Words carry meaning when you know where they come from. Explore festivals, stories, and traditions that shaped Nepal's languages.

🏮
Festival
Indra Jatra — इन्द्र जात्रा
The eight-day festival of the Newar people, celebrating the god Indra. Learn the Newari words for offerings, masks, and the chariot procession.
📖
Folktale
The legend of Manjushri
How the Kathmandu Valley was formed — told in both Newari and Nepali, with vocabulary notes throughout to help you learn as you read.
🥁
Tradition
Tamang Selo music
The haunting folk music of the Tamang people — played on the damphu drum and tungna lute. Learn the vocabulary of instruments, dances, and seasonal songs.
🏔️
Proverb
Nepali proverbs
"जहाँ छ धैर्य, त्यहाँ छ जय" — Where there is patience, there is victory. Explore the wisdom packed into Nepal's most beloved sayings.
